    Features

    Ultimate performance. Uncompromised battery life.

    They say life is full of compromises. They say you can’t have high performance and long battery life on the same notebook. They say that a high-performance gaming laptop has to be big, bulky, and heavy. They’re wrong. Now you can enjoy elite gaming performance on a thin and light notebook with amazing battery life, thanks to the new AMD Ryzen™ 5000 H-Series Mobile Processors. Never compromise on your gaming laptop again.

    Ultimate performance for gamers and creators

    NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 30 Series Laptop GPUs deliver the ultimate performance for gamers and creators. They’re powered by Ampere—NVIDIA’s 2nd Gen RTX architecture—with new Ray Tracing Cores, Tensor Cores, and streaming multiprocessors for a huge leap in performance. Experience the most realistic ray-traced graphics, cutting-edge new features, and the power of AI in thin and light designs.

    Xbox Game Pass. Discover your favorite game.

    Play over 100 high-quality PC games for one low monthly price. EA Play is included and gives you more from the games you love with access to top Electronic Arts titles on PC, more rewards, and more exclusive content! Use the Xbox app on PC to download and play new games the same day they release like Halo Infinite. With games added all the time, you'll always have something new to play.

    Next-level thermal control

    The Legion 5 (15, AMD) is cool and quiet thanks to Legion Coldfront 3.0. Extending its legacy of max clock speeds with zero throttling over long sessions, the latest version ups the stakes even further with an intelligent intake system, a dual fan design with quad-channel exhaust systems and improved thermal fins, and intake vents underneath each keyboard switch and heat sinks. A new intelligent mode uses AI to keep the most demanding games running smoothly and lag-free, while Q Control 4.0 lets you pick between turbo-charged power or extended battery life.

    Behold the Legion AI Engine

    The Legion AI Engine is our most groundbreaking gaming solution yet. Included in the Legion 5 (15, AMD), its Auto-Optimization mode intelligently identifies your game launches and optimizes system performances with dynamic CPU/GPU power distribution. The result is the highest possible FPS whether your game is CPU or GPU intensive. In Auto-Detect mode, you’ll enjoy maximum frame rates on 16 of the most popular AAA titles, exclusive to Legion devices.

    Fast refresh for fast reflexes

    Whether you like blindingly fast refresh rates and response times on your gaming screen or hyper-realistic visuals, the Legion 5 (15, AMD) has you covered. Deepen your immersion with a 15" FHD widescreen display, engineered for the graphical pyrotechnics of up to Dolby Vision™ and 100% sRGB color accuracy. or enhance your competitive edge with blistering refresh speeds of up to 165Hz with 3ms response times via Overdrive support.

    Pinpoint decision

    Tactile. Responsive. Accurate. The Legion 5 (15, AMD) features the Legion TrueStrike keyboard.
